About

This site is licensed under the MIT free software license, and is accessible through our github.

Mangaloid operates as a network of instances, each serving a subset of manga. Our example interface pulls all it's metadata from the REST api of one of these hosts. You are free to host your own instance or interface as you see fit.

Images are stored and loaded in a distributed manner via a number of IPFS nodes and gateways hosted by volunteers. The easiest way to improve loading times is to host your own node.

Feel free to stop by our IRC channel if you have questions: irc.rizon.net #mangaloid
